ABOUT

E-Cell IIT Guwahati is delighted to announce the 2nd Edition of Startup Odyssey, bringing forth a one-of-a-kind opportunity to develop entrepreneurial skills!

Startup Odyssey is a month-long series of online webinars on the weekends by Entrepreneurs, Venture Capitalists, and Startup professionals explaining the various stages of a startup and weekly hands-on exercises to engage the participants by refreshing the concepts covered in the webinar.

Initiate your innovation, fire up your creative engines and start brainstorming as we prepare to traverse the cosmos of entrepreneurship.

A free of cost one stop certified course to develop entrepreneurial skills.
